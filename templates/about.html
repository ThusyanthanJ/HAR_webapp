About

This web application allows users to upload their videos captured in low light conditions. The application utilizes advanced technology to recognize human actions within the uploaded videos, providing users with valuable insights into the content of their videos.

Our goal is to provide users with a seamless experience in uploading their videos and obtaining accurate predictions about human actions, regardless of the lighting conditions. We aim to deliver reliable results and valuable information to our users, enabling them to make informed decisions based on the content of their videos.
