Video Upload and Processing

The website allows users to upload video files (specifically in the .mp4 format) for processing and analysis.

Action Recognition

The application utilizes an LRCN (Long-term Recurrent Convolutional Networks) model to recognize human actions within the uploaded videos.

Predictions and Insights

Users can receive accurate predictions about the actions performed in the uploaded videos, providing valuable insights into the content of their videos.

Video Preprocessing

The application pre-processes the uploaded videos, adjusts brightness, and applies gamma correction to enhance the quality of the video content.

Video Preview

Users can preview the processed videos on the website, enabling them to view the original video with the predicted action overlaid.

Download Processed Videos

The website provides an option to download the processed videos for users to keep a copy of the analyzed content.
